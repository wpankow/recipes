2/3 cup Hershey Cocoa
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup margarine
  2 cups granulated sugar
  2 Tbs. Light Karo Syrup
    Dash of salt

Melt margarine in medium sauce pan. 
Add all other ingredients and mix.
Bring to boil and stir while boiling for 1 minute.
Remove from stove and add:

  4 cups confectionary sugar
  2 tsp. Vanilla
  1 cup chopped walnuts  (NOT OPTIONAL! -W.)

Beat well and then pour into buttered 8 or 9 inch square pan.
  (Doubled - 11 x 15 inch cookie sheet with 1 inch lip)
Let cool completely.
